Frequently Asked Questions

Get to know more about how our system can simplify your quiz creation and management. Here are some frequently asked questions about the platform.

How can I create a group and invite friends or students?

Creating a group is simple! You can easily invite friends or students to join your group. Once invited, participants can accept or reject the invitation. After that, you can assign quizzes to the group and monitor their progress.

How do I track student participation in quizzes?

As an administrator, you have full control to track student participation. You can see if students have participated in quizzes, and check their results. This ensures you know who completed the quiz and their performance.

Can users participate in multiple quizzes?

Each user can participate in a quiz only once per group. This ensures fair participation and allows you to track accurate results for each participant in your group.

How can I generate quizzes quickly?

If you don't have time to create quizzes one by one, you can easily generate quizzes in seconds. Our system allows you to create quizzes for any subject, in any language, with just a few clicks. Once generated, you can review, update, and share the quizzes with your group members.

Can I add questions offline?

Yes, you can! If you prefer to work offline, simply download our Excel template, fill it with your questions and answers, and upload it back into the system. Our platform will process your file, allowing you to review and finalize the questions before saving them to the database.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, simply click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will be prompted to enter your email address, and a password reset link will be sent to you. Follow the link to reset your password and regain access to your account.
